# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, constructive, and generous.

We want AgentHub Network to be a place where people can discuss ideas, report bugs, suggest improvements, and contribute without being harassed or demeaned.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good intent, but stay clear and direct.
- Critique ideas, code, and decisions — not people.
- Be welcoming to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Keep feedback specific and useful.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith engagement
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

If you need to raise a conduct concern, use the contact path in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) and mark it clearly as a conduct issue.
